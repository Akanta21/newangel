OUR MISSION


Angels of Impact believes that we can end poverty and give the poor control of their lives by supporting social enterprises to grow into sustainable businesses. By focussing on women, A.I. aims to maximise the social impact of your money. And by working with enterprises which produce responsibly, you can help businesses which are helping the environment. It's about getting the most bang for your buck.

OUR APPROACH


Our investors are focused on the long-term success of the social businesses that A.I. funds. So they give long-term, low-interest loans to entrepreneurs who are too big for micro-finance but too small for more traditional investors. When the loans are repaid the money can be re-invested into other social enterprises. We call this ‘enterprise philanthropy’: money you can give again and again.

OUR GOALS


We believe passionately that together we can achieve these goals:

  • Responsible Consumption
  • Gender Equality
  • Eradication of poverty
